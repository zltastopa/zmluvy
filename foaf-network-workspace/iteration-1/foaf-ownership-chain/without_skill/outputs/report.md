## 4. Key Findings

1. **Ultimate beneficial owner:** **Karel Komarek**, Czech billionaire entrepreneur, controls Aricoma Systems s.r.o. through a multi-layered corporate chain: KKCG -> Aricoma Group -> Aricoma Systems a.s. -> Aricoma Systems s.r.o.

2. **Former identity:** The Slovak entity was historically connected to AUTOCONT a.s. / AutoCont Holding, a.s., all at the same Ostrava address. The AUTOCONT brand was absorbed into the Aricoma Group identity.

3. **Concentration risk:** 70.7% of the total CRZ contract value (6.99M EUR out of 9.89M EUR) comes from a single contract with the Ministry of Interior.

4. **Legitimate corporate structure:** The ownership chain is typical for a large Central European IT group. KKCG is a well-known, established investment group. The minority shareholders in the Slovak entity are Aricoma employees/managers holding incentive stakes, which is standard practice.

5. **Scale context:** Aricoma Systems s.r.o. (Slovak subsidiary) reported revenues of approximately 80.3M EUR (2024) with profit of about 1.6M EUR. The 9.9M EUR in CRZ government contracts represents roughly 12% of the Slovak entity's revenue.
